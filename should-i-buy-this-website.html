How it works

Scan. Decide. Save.

Three steps between you and a smarter purchase decision.

01
📷
Scan the barcode
Point your camera at any EAN, UPC, or QR code. Works on groceries, electronics, cosmetics, clothing — anything with a barcode.
02
💷
Enter the price
Optionally tell us what they're charging. AI can still give a verdict without it, but the more context, the sharper the advice.
03
🧠
Get your verdict
GPT-4o analyses the product, your purchase history, and the price. You get a clear Buy / Skip / Maybe with a score out of 100 and honest reasoning.
Features

shop without regret.

Clear Buy / Skip / Maybe verdicts
No wishy-washy advice. Every scan gets a definitive verdict with a buy score from 0 to 100 and plain-English reasoning you can act on instantly.

Purchase memory
The app remembers every product you've scanned. Buying Pringles for the 6th time this month? AI will notice and factor that into its verdict.

Smarter alternatives
When you should skip something, AI suggests what to buy instead — with estimated savings so you know exactly how much better the alternative is.

Nutri-Score aware
For food products, the AI considers nutritional quality (Nutri-Score A–E) alongside price, so you're not just saving money — you're eating better too.

Your scan history & savings tracker
Every scan is saved. Browse your full history grouped by date, filter by verdict, search by product name. See at a glance how much money you've saved by following AI's "skip" verdicts.
